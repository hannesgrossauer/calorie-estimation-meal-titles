Task: Convert a constructed meal title into a plausible ingredient list with quantities for one plated dish.

Important:
The meal title is not a natural dish name. It is already a short list of the most important ingredients in the dish.
Example:
"pork, brown rice, olive oil and sugar"

Rules:
- Do not browse the web.
- Treat the listed items as the core ingredients already present in the dish.
- Keep the ingredient list close to the title.
- Do not add many new ingredients.
- Add an ingredient only if it is necessary to make the dish coherent.
- Do not add sides, garnish, sauces, or optional extras unless strongly implied.
- Use moderate, realistic quantities for one plated dish.
- Be conservative with high-calorie ingredients such as oils, nuts, cheese, bacon, and sugar.
- Each ingredient must have:
  - name
  - quantity
  - unit
- Use "g" for most solid ingredients.
- Use "ml" for liquids.
- Use "piece" only for discrete items.
- Estimate total_mass_g as the approximate edible mass of the whole dish.
- Return only valid JSON matching the schema.
