Task: Estimate the total calories of a single plated dish from a constructed meal title.

Important:
The meal title is not a natural recipe name. It is a short list of the most important ingredients in one dish, for example:
"pork, brown rice, olive oil and sugar"

Rules:
- Do not browse the web.
- Interpret the title as a list of salient ingredients from one actual plated dish.
- Do not assume a large restaurant portion unless clearly implied.
- Do not invent many extra ingredients beyond what is listed.
- Use the listed ingredients as the main basis for the estimate.
- Assume a moderate, realistic portion size for one dish.
- Be conservative with very calorie-dense ingredients such as oils, nuts, cheese, bacon, and sugar unless the title strongly suggests a large amount.
- Return only valid JSON matching the schema.
